Seminyak Beach

1. Crystal Palace Beach Hut

(Legian)

Right next door to The Champlung is Crystal Palace. It's among the earlier beach bars to open along the Double Six beach strip. The main restaurant building has an upstairs deck with table setups. Here, you can enjoy the wonderful sunset views and drink or dine under the stars. The beachfront, with its beanbags and parasols, is more popular. Off the menu, you can have a wide selection of seafood and meat grills, as well as lighter meals like spring rolls and crispy calamari. A small Bintang is IDR 25,000 (USD 1.80), while special cocktail selections start from IDR 35,000 (USD 2.60). Live bands playing Top 40 songs serenade the beachfront almost nightly.

Opening Hours: 07:00-24:00
Location: Jalan Arjuna (Double Six), Legian.
Tel: +62 (0)813 3878 9494

2. Bali Juice Park Bar & Grill

(Seminyak)

Near the northernmost part of Double Six Beach, Bali Juice Park offers beachfront dining and a casual bar scene. The menu comprises authentic local Indonesian favourites. These include nasi goreng fried rice, soto ayam chicken soup and gado-gado salad. Popular light bites range from chicken wings, to lumpia spring rolls and calamari fries. The drink list also features quite a mix. You have chocies of healthy tropical fruit juices (from IDR 35,000), fresh-brewed coffee (from IDR 20,000) and different beers. A small Bintang is IDR 35,000. If that’s not enough, you can also go for a Bintang tower at IDR 250,000 (USD 19). There are many cocktails to choose from, including Arak Madu (honey arak) at IDR 40,000.

Opening Hours: 09:00-23:00
Location: Jalan Camplung Tanduk (Dhyana Pura), Seminyak.
Tel: +62 (0)878 7145 6019

3. The Sand Beach Bar & Grill

(Legian)

The Sand is both a beach bar and a restaurant, just north of Blue 9 Beach Bar. It’s great for lunch with a view or for drinks at sunset. You can hit its sheltered dining space, or go for the colourful beanbags under mini Balinese umbrellas on the sand. By day, it’s good for watching the surfing action with a cold beer and pastas or pizzas (from IDR 85,000 or USD 6.50). Classic cocktails, such as Mai Tais and Margaritas, start from IDR 100,000 per glass and there’s nightly live music.

Opening Hours: 08:00-24:00
Location: Jalan Arjuna (Double Six), Legian.
Tel: +62 (0)878 6288 9129

4. Capil Beach Bar & Grill

(Seminyak)

This bar's right on the beach in front of the magnificent Double Six Luxury Hotel. Capil Beach Bar is a great place to meet and enjoy an early evening drink at sunset. The cosy beanbags usually get filled up quick, so make sure you come a little early. Local bands play live regularly from a stage right on the sand. They'll perform anything from ‘80s rock and Top 40s to special requests. If you don’t mind the beach peddlers constantly trying to sell you trinkets of all sorts, you can have a memorable sunset chill-out experience here. Capil’s signature nibbles (from IDR 40,000 or USD 3) include nachos, quesadillas and prawn tempura. These go well with a small Bintang (IDR 35,000 or USD 2.60) or refreshing signature cocktails, such as the Green Capil (IDR 95,000) and Passion Dream (IDR 100,000).

Opening Hours: 08:00-24:00
Location: Jalan Double Six, Seminyak.
Tel: +62 (0)859 5378 4779

5. 707 Beachberm Batubelig

(Kerobokan)

This laidback beach bar is on the dark sand coast between Seminyak and Canggu. It's far north from the bustle of Legian’s Double Six strip. The Beachberm is quite popular for sunsets, drinks and groovy music. Its live DJs play almost nightly from a raised booth. As seating goes, you have the options of sinking into the colourful beanbags on the lawn or on a raised bamboo platform. You can even go further down at the wide parasol-shaded sunbeds. The kitchen is quite basic, serving simple ‘surfers’ bites’. These include fried calamari, nasi goreng fried rice and mie goreng fried noodles. However, the chill-out ambiance is quite lively, and the no-frills service is part of the reason crowds gather here. A small Bintang is IDR 25,000 (USD 1.85) and cocktails are from IDR 95,000 (USD 7).

Opening Hours: 10:00-21:00
Location: Jalan Batubelig No.33 A, Kerobokan.
Tel: +62 (0)812 3981 356
